Pursuant to Neb. Ct. R. App. P. §§ 2-112 and 2-102(E)(3), the only official version of the opinions of the Nebraska Supreme Court and the Nebraska Court of Appeals appears in the bound volumes of the Nebraska Reports and the Nebraska Appellate Reports.

POSTING TIMES

The slip opinions and lists released by the Nebraska Supreme Court and the Nebraska Court of Appeals in the last 90 days are posted here in full text (in PDF). The Supreme Court opinions, which are filed 8 a.m. Friday mornings, and the Court of Appeals opinions, which are filed 8 a.m. Tuesday mornings, are posted to this site between 8 a.m. and 6 p.m. (Central Time) on the day on which they are released.

SUBSCRIPTIONS

Yearly subscriptions to the Nebraska Advance Sheets and the Decisions of the Nebraska Court of Appeals are available. Click here for Nebraska Advance Sheets subscription form and information; click here for Decisions of the Nebraska Court of Appeals subscription form and information. Current and past bound volumes of Nebraska Reports and Nebraska Appellate Reports are also available; the most current volumes available are 274 Nebraska Reports and 14 Nebraska Appellate Reports. Contact the Publications Office for more information or to place an order for bound volumes.
