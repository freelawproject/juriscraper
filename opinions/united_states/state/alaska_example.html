Opinions are removed from this site once they are printed in the Pacific Reporter, the designated official reporter of Alaskan appellate decisions. Opinions from 1960 to present can also be found in the law library's Alaska Case Law database.

Supreme court slip opinions are subject to correction before publication in the Pacific Reporter. Please bring errors to the attention of: Clerk of the Appellate Courts, 303 K Street, Anchorage, Alaska 99501, 907-264-0608, 907-264-0878 (fax), corrections@appellate.courts.state.ak.us.

Opinions are provided in Adobe Acrobat PDF format. You will need to download a free copy of Adobe Acrobat Reader in order to view and print appellate court opinions and MOJs. If you are not able to access any of the files linked from this web page, please contact the Appellate Clerk's Office at 907-264-0612 for assistance.
