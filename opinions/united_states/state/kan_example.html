Opinions Date of Release List

Cite published opinions using the official citations followed by any generally recognized reporter citation System. SCR 6.08

Kansas Supreme Court and Court of Appeals opinions are normally released each Friday. Published Appellate Opinions are generally posted to this website within one hour of the time (usually 9:30 a.m. each Friday) they are transmitted by the Reporter's Office to the webmaster. Beginning Friday, October 2, 2009, opinions are posted in PDF format. You need Adobe Acrobat Reader 5.0 or greater to view and print these documents.  The software is available without charge at www.adobe.com.
