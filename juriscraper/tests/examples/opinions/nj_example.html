Supreme and Appellate Opinions

Listed on this page are opinions issued by the New Jersey Supreme Court and Appellate Division, Superior Court. These opinions will be made available for 10 business days, after 10 business days you may access the opinions through Rutgers Newark Law School. Opinions are posted in Adobe .pdf. format and require the download of Adobe Reader.

Rutgers Newark Law School hosts a searchable database of New Jersey Supreme Court and Appellate Court reported opinions dating from March 1994 to the present. Also on that web site is a full text archive of the opinions of the Advisory Committee on Professional Ethics, Committee on the Unauthorized Practice of Law, and the Committee on Attorney Advertising.
