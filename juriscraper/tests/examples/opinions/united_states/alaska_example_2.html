Recent Supreme Court Slip Opinions

Opinions are removed from this site once they are printed in the Pacific Reporter, the designated official reporter of Alaskan appellate decisions. Opinions from 1960 to present can also be found in the law library's Alaska Case Law Service.

Supreme court slip opinions are subject to correction before publication in the Pacific Reporter. Please bring errors to the attention of:
