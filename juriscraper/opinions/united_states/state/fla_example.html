    Websites Cited in Florida Supreme Court Opinions

    The Clerk's office releases weekly opinions at approximately 11 a.m. each Thursday. The Court makes every effort to post them to the web site as soon as possible thereafter. Additionally, there may be times when opinions are released outside of this schedule.

    Opinions are provided based on the year the opinion was released. Opinions are made available online on this web site from September 24, 1999 to the present. The Florida Supreme Court also has information regarding archived opinions.

    Weekly Opinion Release in PDF Format. Click here for help and information on file formats and downloading.

    Note: Unless opinions say otherwise, they are not final until any timely filed motions for rehearing are considered and disposed of by the Court.  These opinions are also subject to formal revision before publication in the Southern Reporter, 3rd Series.
