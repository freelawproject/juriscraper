Recent Court of Appeals Slip Opinions

Opinions are removed from this site once they are printed in the Pacific Reporter, the designated official reporter of Alaskan appellate decisions. Opinions from 1980 to present can also be found in the law library's Alaska Case Law Service.

Court of Appeals slip opinions are subject to correction before publication in the Pacific Reporter. Please bring errors to the attention of:

Clerk of the Appellate Courts
303 K Street
Anchorage AK 99501
phone: 907-264-0608
fax: 907-264-0878
email: corrections@akcourts.us
