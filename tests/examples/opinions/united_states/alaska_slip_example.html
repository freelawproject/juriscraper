Case-Related Orders are published weekly on Fridays at 9AM. If you have visited this page and not refreshed prior to 9AM Friday, please refresh to see the latest postings. The documents are available for direct download until they are published in the official reporter of Alaska appellate decisions - Pacific Reporter(P.2d and P.3d), and the Alaska Reporter, which contains the Alaska cases excerpted from P.2d and P.3d. The Pacific Reporter or the Alaska Reporter is available at most Alaska Court System law libraries and some public libraries.
