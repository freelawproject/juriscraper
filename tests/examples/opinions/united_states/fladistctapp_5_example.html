    Fifth District Court of Appeal Opinions

    Most Recent Written Opinions | Most Recent PCAs | Opinions Archive

    The Court issues written opinions on Fridays; those opinions are posted to the website before noon on Friday.  The Court issues Per Curiam Affirmance(PCA ) decisions on Tuesdays.  The Court may issue opinions or PCAs on other days of the week if it is deemed necessary by the Court.
