Attorney General Opinions

Online and Printed Opinions

This database of Attorney General opinions is provided for your convenience. Differences, if any, between online and printed versions are resolved in favor of the printed copy.

For official printed copies by mail or email, call us at 503-947-4540. Pre-payment is required for any copying and mailing costs.

Formal and Informal Opinions

The opinions fall into two broad categories:

  • Formal Opinions
    • Address constitutional issues and matters of statewide concern
    • Are signed by the Attorney General
    • Are listed in the subject index with a four-digit number, i.e. “OP 8246”
  • Informal Opinions
    • Are signed by the Chief Counsel of the Department of Justice General Counsel Division
    • Are listed in the subject index with a year and dash, i.e. “OP 1997-1”
