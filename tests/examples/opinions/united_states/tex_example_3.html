24-0171
MAYA WALNUT LLC F/K/A MAYA FOODS, INC. v. BRYAN LY, WALNUT CREEK CENTER, INC., LENG CHIV LY, AND SAO MINH LY; from Dallas County; 5th Court of Appeals District (05-21-01140-CV, 719 SW3d 347, 01-24-24)
 
The Court affirms the court of appeals' judgment and remands the case to the trial court.

Justice Busby delivered the opinion of the Court, in which Justice Devine, Justice Bland, Justice Huddle, Justice Young, and Justice Hawkins joined.

Chief Justice Blacklock filed an opinion concurring in the judgment, in which Justice Lehrmann and Justice Sullivan joined.

24-0543
FAMILY DOLLAR STORES OF TEXAS, LLC, ARCP FDCCC1403 LLC, 7B BUILDING & DEVELOPMENT, LLC, TRIPLE C DEVELOPMENT, INC., BURKHARDT ENGINEERING COMPANY, AND M&S UTILITY CONSTRUCTION, LLC v. JLMH INVESTMENTS, LLC; from Tarrant County; 2nd Court of Appeals District (02-23-00233-CV, 716 SW3d 770, 06-13-24)
 
The Court reverses the court of appeals' judgment and reinstates the trial court's judgment.

Justice Busby delivered the opinion of the Court with respect to Part II, in which Chief Justice Blacklock, Justice Lehrmann, Justice Devine, Justice Bland, Justice Huddle, and Justice Young joined, and an opinion with respect to Part I, in which Justice Lehrmann and Justice Devine joined.

Justice Busby filed a concurring opinion, in which Justice Lehrmann and Justice Devine joined.

Justice Young filed an opinion concurring in part and in the judgment, in which Justice Bland and Justice Huddle joined, and in which Chief Justice Blacklock joined as to Part II.

Justice Sullivan filed a dissenting opinion, in which Chief Justice Blacklock and Justice Hawkins joined.

Justice Young filed a concurring opinion, in which Justice Devine joined.

Chief Justice Blacklock filed an opinion concurring in part and dissenting in part, in which Justice Bland joined.
